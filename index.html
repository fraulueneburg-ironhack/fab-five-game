THE FAB FIVE GAME!

  • ?

How it works:

There are five objects in different colors.
For each round, draw a card that shows two objects in two different colors.
Now, be quick and click the object that is shown in its original color.
If none of the objects are depicted in their original color,
grab the object whose shape or color cannot be found on the card.
